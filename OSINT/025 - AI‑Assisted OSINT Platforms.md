# 🤖 AI‑Assisted OSINT Platforms

**Intro:**  
AI-assisted OSINT platforms leverage **machine learning, generative AI, and automated analysis** to accelerate investigations. These tools help analysts process large datasets, verify content authenticity, and extract patterns that would be difficult to detect manually.

---

## 🔗 Resources & Quick Guide

- 🧩 **[Cylect](https://www.cylect.io/)**  
  *What:* AI-powered entity extraction and link-analysis workspace.  
  *Why use it:* Automatically identify connections between people, organizations, and events.  

- ⚡ **[Fivecast Matrix](https://www.fivecast.com/products/matrix/)**  
  *What:* Generative AI for social media triage and risk scoring.  
  *Why use it:* Quickly assess large social media datasets for threats, trends, or suspicious activity.

- ✅ **[C2PA](https://c2patool.pixelstream.com/)**  
  *What:* Verify if an image was generated via AI.  
  *Why use it:* Pair with Adobe Content Credentials Verify to authenticate digital media.  
  *Quick tip:* Essential for identifying manipulated or AI-generated imagery.

- 🚗 **[CarNet](https://carnet.ai/)**  
  *What:* AI tool to identify car models from images.  
  *Why use it:* Useful in investigations involving vehicles in photos or videos.

- 🌐 **[SkyView](https://bsky.jazco.dev/)** (or similar Bluesky explorers)  
  *What:* Explore Mastodon/Bluesky instances and social graphs.  
  *Why use it:* Analyze social network activity, trends, and user connections efficiently.
